Read our story

Omoni Restaurant, we're tastier..fresher..classier!

Omoni Cuisine is a New inter-continental restaurant and bar located in Lekki phase 1, Lagos. From its industrial luxury design elements to its hidden bar and lounge behind the restaurant, Omoni is inspired by the prohibition era restaurants and bars in Manhattan NYC.

Great freshly made food, an excellent bar program and exceptional service, along with carefully curated music and world class design were elements that set the new standards in the city. Omoni is an upscale casual restaurant that has never compromised on its fundamental promise to its clients. Read more..
